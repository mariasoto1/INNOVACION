PERUVIAN COMPANY MANUFACTURERS OF FOLDABLE FURNITURE: MOSPACIO PERÚ

Peruvian company that makes folding beds of different types.

The folding beds are manufactured in both individual size and for marriage, and therefore are the favorite to accommodate any space. With an artifact of these characteristics you can fold it for the day and place it on the floor at night

TIPS FOR CHOOSING YOUR FOLDING BED: Surely your biggest concern is that the bed is not adjusted and can fall suddenly, these folding furniture has great strength and its mechanisms have been tested for proper operation.

These furniture have various features such as the type of opening, the thickness of the mattress and the safety ladder or railing.
