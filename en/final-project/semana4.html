Presentation Part 1

Procedure to assemble the folding furniture.

To assemble this piece of furniture, it took a lot of wooden machines, which helped us to give it a better finish and obtain the shapes we wanted. Next we will mention the steps that were carried out for the assembly of this piece of furniture.

1.- We have to have the complete wooden parts and according to the necessary measure, which preferably are exact.

2.- We proceed to sand the wood, until it is quite smooth.

3.- We started with the base of 77x55 which is unique to the outside of the woods with measures of 77x12 and 60x12. For this part, the drill is used to screw.

4.- Once we have the surface of the sides, we continue to join the 57x10 woods at the bottom using screws and nails to obtain a higher joining hardness.

5.- Continue to place the 2-inch and 3/4-inch hinges, again using the drill.

6.- Finally, all the parts have to be joined, cutting the piece that will go behind the piece of furniture in order to be recessed, it had to be cut at 45 °.
